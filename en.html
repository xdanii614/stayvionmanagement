Short-term rentals typically involve more frequent turnovers and more operational steps. Mid-term rentals (1‚Äì6 months) are calmer, with fewer turnovers and often more predictable. In the first call, we recommend the best model for your property.

We work with fixed cleaning workflows and checklists. Depending on the model, we schedule cleaning and quality checks aligned with turnovers or rental intervals. If we find issues or damages, we report them immediately and coordinate the solution.

Is there a minimum contract term?

No fixed minimum term. Many owners start with a 3-month test phase to set up workflows properly. Notice periods are agreed transparently in the contract.
